About Filmplex

Filmplex is a leading entertainment company in the cinema industry. Although Filmplex is young, it has become one of the most popular top-tier brands that sells film tickets and offering digital renting service in Canada. Filmplex is extremely popular with youth because of offering the high quality of service, operating innovative business model, always offering lower price products, and most importantly, providing dynamic activities and events of films. In 2016, filmplex has sold over 50 million tickets all around the Canada through its website. What’s more, renting film section has reached 20 million users. Filmplex also provided 50 events during 2016 which has increased by 10% when compared with the quantities of 2015. Filmplex is proud of what it has accomplish and appreciate for its users. It promises to do its best job to meet customers’ needs by maintain the service quality and improving continuously.

History of Filmplex

Filmplex was founded in 2005 by 3 film lovers: Adam Ewing, Robert Frobisher, and Luisa Rey. Their website became a success in 2007 because of its creativity on business model and its special style. It started from selling tickets of independent films and small-budget film while cooperating with small cinemas. It sold tickets online and it provided certain activities which absorbed the youth. It also organized several events to gathering film fans and hosted it as a group. Renting service is another specialty of its business model. After accumulating tickets points and event tickets, users could rent films online for free. With its quick expansion during 2009-2011, it has become the biggest website tickets seller in Canada. Now, Filmplex is one of the most popular film website in Canada.

Today’s Filmplex

Filmplex is headquartered in Toronto, Canada, and cooperates with 100 theatres with 800 screens all over the Canada. Its website has one million visitors a day. Around 35 million users rent film through it. Moreover, several famous worldwide events has been hosted through it.
